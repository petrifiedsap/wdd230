More than just scooters...

As a locally owned and operated business, our mission is simple: to provide you with the freedom to explore Cozumel at your own pace. We offer a wide range of rental options— Honda scooters and ATVs, and 2- and 4-door Jeeps—that allow you to discover everything this tropical paradise has to offer, from hidden beaches to scenic viewpoints and vibrant local spots.

With years of experience and a deep love for the island, our team is dedicated to ensuring you have a seamless and unforgettable experience. Our fleet of well-maintained, high-quality vehicles is ready to take you wherever your adventure leads, whether that's off-road or cruising along the shore.

At Scoots, we’re here to help you make the most of your visit with personalized service, expert recommendations, and everything you need for a safe and exciting ride. Let your Cozumel adventure begin!
