Rates & Payment

  • Rentals are charged per half-day or full-day.
  • Fuel is not included in the rental price.
  • A refundable damage deposit is required (amount varies by vehicle type).

Insurance & Liability

  • Basic liability insurance is included in the rental fee.
  • Optional damage waiver is available for an additional charge.
  • The renter is responsible for:
    • Damage or loss of the vehicle
    • Traffic violations or fines incurred during rental
    • Injury to passengers or third parties (unless covered by insurance)

Use of Vehicle

  • Vehicles may be used on paved roads only (ATVs may be used off-road in designated areas).
  • Off-roading is prohibited for scooters and Jeeps.
  • Helmets must be worn at all times (provided with rental).
  • Driving under the influence of drugs or alcohol is strictly prohibited.

Return Policy

  • Vehicles must be returned on time and in the same condition as received.
  • Late returns may incur additional fees (half-day or full-day rate).
  • Lost or damaged items (keys, helmets, etc.) will be charged at replacement value.

Cancellations & Refunds

  • Full refunds are available for cancellations made 24 hours or more in advance.
  • No refunds will be issued for:
    • Same-day cancellations
    • Early returns
