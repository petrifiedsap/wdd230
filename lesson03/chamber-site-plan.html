Minneola Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

I chose this name because the site will be for the Chamber of Commerce for Minneola, TX

Site Purpose

The Minneola Chamber of Commerce website will provide tools and information to the local business community. It will give information about upcoming Chamber events and networking opportunities to help the business community help each other and thrive. The website will also serve the memebers of the Chamber with members-only content and events.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find information about business networking events in my town?
  • Where can I find out what impact our local Chamber is having on our community?
  • What are the benefits of joining my local chamber of commerce?
